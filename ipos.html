Caveats — read first:
  • US listings (NASDAQ/NYSE): sourced systematically from SEC EDGAR 424B4 (final prospectus) filings, filtered to biotech/pharma SIC codes 2830, 2833, 2835, 2836, 8731. True-IPO filter applied: company must have an S-1 on file, and earliest 10-K must be after or within 6 months of the first 424B4. See Overview → Methodology for full details.
  • HKEX Chapter 18A pre-revenue biotech listings: hand-curated from HKEXnews.hk — not automated.
  • Excluded by scope: SPAC mergers (S-4), direct listings, secondary/follow-on offerings.
  • Clinical detail (MOA, lead asset, phase, news) is populated for ~57 originally-curated entries. The ~124 EDGAR-backfilled rows have ticker, raise amount, IPO date, and exchange but blank clinical fields — to be filled in over time.
  • Latest close and market cap are approximate as of 2026-05-24. A weekly GitHub Actions job (scripts/refresh_ipos.py) auto-refreshes these via yfinance once deployed.
  • News column shows factual headlines only — no editorializing. Phase reflects most-advanced asset.
