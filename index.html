Methodology

How each page is sourced, and what's automated vs hand-curated. Read this before citing any specific number.

Pharma Pipelines — fully hand-curated

Top-20 pharma by Rx revenue. No automation; every cell is read off a primary source by hand:

  • Revenue by therapeutic area — company 10-K segment reporting + Q4 earnings call decks (FY2024 figures, rounded to USD billions).
  • Pipeline counts and lead assets — each company's own published pipeline page or most recent R&D-day deck.
  • Patent cliff dates — US composition-of-matter or biologic exclusivity expiry, cross-checked against FDA Orange/Purple Book and analyst aggregations.
  • Boehringer Ingelheim is private — figures are estimates only.

Implication: pipeline data lags whatever each company most recently updated on its own site. Always verify against the source before citing.

M&A Deals — systematic SEC EDGAR pull (acquisitions) + curated trade press (licensing & partnerships)

US public-company acquisitions, all sizes, 2021–2026: sourced systematically by paginating SEC EDGAR's full-text search (EFTS API) for two form types, then filtering to biotech/pharma SIC codes:

  • SC 14D9 — Solicitation/Recommendation Statement filed by the target in response to a tender offer.
  • DEFM14A — Definitive Merger Proxy filed by the target for shareholder vote.
  • SIC code filter: 2830, 2833, 2835, 2836, 8731 (pharmaceuticals, biologicals, in-vitro/in-vivo diagnostics, commercial physical & biological research).

Deal terms (acquirer, price/share, total value, lead asset, MOA, rationale) are then filled in from each filing document and the company press release.

Licensing and Partnership+Equity deals (≥$500M headline only): still hand-curated from trade press (FierceBiotech, BioPharma Dive, Endpoints, company press releases).

Excluded by scope: SPAC business combinations, asset divestitures (not full acquisitions), reverse mergers, CRO/diagnostics-only acquisitions, Concentra-style cash-shell wind-downs.

Script: data/fetch_edgar_ma.py. Intermediate output: data/edgar_ma_pull.json (67 biotech targets discovered).

IPOs — systematic SEC EDGAR pull (US) + hand-curated HKEX 18A

US biotech IPOs, no raise threshold, 2021–2026: sourced systematically by paginating SEC EDGAR for 424B4 (final prospectus) filings, then applying two filters:

  • SIC code filter: same biotech/pharma codes as above (2830, 2833, 2835, 2836, 8731).
  • True-IPO filter (vs follow-on offering): company must have an S-1 registration on file, and the earliest 10-K must be after or within 6 months of the first 424B4.

This catches every confirmed US listing — no judgment call about what's "worth including."

HKEX Chapter 18A pre-revenue biotech listings: hand-curated from HKEXnews.hk.

Excluded by scope: SPACs (S-4 mergers), direct listings, secondary/follow-on offerings.

Script: data/fetch_edgar_ipos.py. Clinical detail (MOA, lead asset, phase) is populated for the ~57 originally-curated rows; the ~124 EDGAR-backfilled rows have ticker, raise, IPO date, and exchange but blank clinical fields — to be filled in over time.

Refresh cadence and known limits
  • Latest close and market cap on the IPO page are approximate as of 2026-05-24, auto-refreshed weekly via yfinance through a GitHub Actions job (once deployed).
  • Acquisition data from EDGAR will not catch private-target acquisitions, since private companies don't file SC 14D9 / DEFM14A. Those are still hand-added.
  • HKEX 18A listings aren't covered by EDGAR — handled separately.
  • Verify before citing in writing. Round numbers are used throughout to keep the table readable.
Sources: SEC EDGAR (424B4, SC 14D9, DEFM14A), company 10-K filings, company pipeline pages, HKEXnews, Endpoints, FierceBiotech, BioPharma Dive. Methodology updated 2026-05-25.
