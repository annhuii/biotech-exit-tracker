Caveats — read first:
  • Acquisitions (US public targets, all sizes): sourced systematically from SEC EDGAR SC 14D9 (target-filed tender offer recommendations) + DEFM14A (target-filed merger proxies), filtered to biotech/pharma SIC codes 2830, 2833, 2835, 2836, 8731. See Overview → Methodology for full details. Backfilled May 2026 — earlier curation was incomplete.
  • Licensing and Partnership+Equity deals: still hand-curated from trade press, ≥$500M headline threshold applies. Below-threshold licensing deals are not tracked here.
  • Excluded by scope: SPAC business combinations, asset divestitures (not full company acquisitions), reverse mergers, CRO/diagnostics-only acquisitions, Concentra-style cash-shell wind-downs.
  • Private-target acquisitions are not in the systematic pull (private companies don't file with SEC) — those rely on trade-press curation and may be incomplete.
  • HKEX-listed targets are not in the EDGAR pull — handled separately.
  • Headline value = max economic value to seller including milestones. For public targets, deal_structure includes $/share + premium %. Round numbers used; verify before citing.
