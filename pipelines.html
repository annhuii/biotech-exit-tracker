Pharma Pipelines — Top 20 by Rx Revenue

Three structured views: who's acquired the most, where each pharma's commercial strength sits today, and how deep each pipeline is.

Caveats — read first:
  • Fully hand-curated, not automated. Pipeline data lags whatever each company most recently posted on its own site — no live scraping.
  • Sources: company 10-K segment reporting (revenue), each company's own published pipeline page or R&D-day deck (pipeline counts and lead assets), FDA Orange/Purple Book + analyst aggregations (patent cliff dates).
  • Revenue figures are approximate FY2024 prescription drug sales by therapeutic area, in USD billions. Rounded for readability — not precision figures.
  • Boehringer Ingelheim is private — figures are external estimates only.
  • Patent cliff dates are US composition-of-matter / biologic exclusivity unless otherwise noted.
  • Verify against the company's own source before citing any specific number.
